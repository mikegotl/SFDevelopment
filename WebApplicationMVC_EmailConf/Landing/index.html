BookYour Best Speech Ever

Finally! A speech book that awakens and enlightens developing speakers at any level. Your Best Speech Ever examines the fear, dread and phobias public speaking often evokes in people and examines the stress or anxiety response many experience. Specific strategies are outlined to conquer the fear and speak confidently to audiences of any size or speeches for any purpose

Author J.R. Steele’s systematic approach is a breath of fresh air to speakers of any level. Each chapter highlights practice speeches designed to reinforce the principles, build technique and confidence.

Part 2: Write Your Best Speech Ever!

Leave your audience spellbound!

Creating a speech involves a rather complicated process. We help you meet this challenge by providing a Speech Formula for constructing the speech – an application that guides you through each step of the process. When you reach the end of the prompts, the speech, slides and cards are ready! This workshop will reveal the Speech Formula that will empower you to write your best speech ever!

  • Conduct Audience Analysis(s)
  • Create a clear purpose
  • Select the best organizational strategy
  • Write a skeleton outline
  • Insert credible sources and engaging connections
  • Prepare slides & cards
  • Prepare for delivery
  • Exam

As you progress through the program, each step contains interactions requiring a response. The program saves the responses, which are then used to generate a completed speech. When you reach the final step, your speech outline, notecards and visuals are completed. A final WORD document is generated.

How does the speech application work?

The Speech Formula Application guides you through a series of 8 steps which follows our proven FORMULA for delivering a dynamic speech. As you progress through the program, each step contains interactions requiring a response. The program saves the responses, which are then used to generate a completed speech. When you reach the final step, your speech outline, slides and notecards are completed. It’s that easy! Speech Formula works for most any individual, instructor, manager, or organization and will transform their capacity to communicate effectively with others.

  • Step 1: Create your profile.
  • Step 2: Select the right track for you!
  • Step 3: Respond to each engaging interaction for each track.
  • Step 4: Enjoy the benefits of communicating clearly and confidently!
